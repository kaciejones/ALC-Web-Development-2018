Awards and Accomplishments

State FFA Officer Candidate Final 12

2018

I had the opportunity to represent the Spanish Fork FFA Chapter as a state officer candidate. In March, at state convention, I went through a series of rigorous interviews and made it to the top twelve.

Spanish Fork FFA Farmers Market Manager

2015-2018

For part of my FFA Project, I work at the Spanish Fork Greenhouse each summer. In the fall I help manage the Farmers Market Stand, coordinating workers and keeping inventory.

Spanish Fork FFA President

2017-2018

For the 2017-2018 school year I served as the Spanish Fork FFA President. This leadership position allowed me to plan activities, delegate meetings, and represent the chapter.

Utah FFA Vegetable Production Proficiency Winner

2018

I am a state winner for Vegetable Production. I recieved this award for my FFA Project, as I kept accurate records of work and finances. In October I will represent the state of Utah at the 90th annual National Convention.

Utah FFA State Floral Team

2018

As a team we placed 3rd out of 47 teams from accross the state of Utah. This was a big accomplishment, and we spent many hours preparing for state contests. As an individual I took 1st place overall, and had the top flower ID score.

Sterling Scholar in Skilled and Technical Science

2018

I was chosen as the Sterling Scholar for Spanish Fork High School. I displayed all of my achievements within CTE and Agriculture and interviewed at the region level.
